# 🚀 Support Engineer Lab

> A hands-on learning repository focused on PostgreSQL, Supabase, troubleshooting, incident response, and real-world Support Engineering.

This project documents my journey toward becoming a world-class Support Engineer by combining database internals, SaaS troubleshooting, customer support workflows, and production incident management.

---

## 🎯 Goals

* Develop deep PostgreSQL expertise
* Master Supabase architecture and troubleshooting
* Practice real-world incident response
* Build a portfolio of support case studies
* Prepare for Support Engineer interviews at companies such as Supabase, TigerData (Timescale), PostHog, Grafana, and other developer-focused platforms
